We are The Drunk Pawn

Welcome to The Drunk Pawn, a vibrant Chess + Beer cafe that skillfully combines intellectual stimulation with social enjoyment. Whether you're a seasoned chess enthusiast or a casual player, our lively haven invites you to gather, unwind, and engage in friendly yet competitive matches of the world's greatest game. The symphony of clinking chess pieces harmonizes with the laughter of patrons, fostering an ambiance that not only encourages camaraderie but also stimulates strategic thinking.

What does The Drunk Pawn offer?

  • The Drunk Pawn offers people a fun and enjoyable atmosphere to come hang out with friends and meet new and interesting people while enjoying a glass of beer and playing games of chess.
  • Provides a space where games of chess can be played face to face and on a physical board. Often players only option is to play online as it is difficult to find people willing to play the game or even have a board to play on.
  • Serves a number of products that satisfy the customer base such as to-go beer cans, chess boards and accessories, merch, etc.
